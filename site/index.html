Corpus Atlas

Open African-language data, routed with provenance intact.

Pandora distinguishes importable open corpora from research-only, share-alike, gated, and evaluation-only datasets before anything enters the saleable `model_training` lake.

Translation

Setswana, Igbo, Kinyarwanda

Parallel text lanes with row-level source IDs, license tags, and upstream citations.

Sentiment

Hausa, Yoruba, Swahili

Classification rows imported as generic public-corpus records for downstream benchmarks.

Speech

Setswana, Sepedi, Xhosa, Yoruba

Transcript-first ASR lanes, ready for audio mirroring when a stable storage bucket is assigned.

Governance

Restricted sources held out

Evaluation-only, non-commercial, and no-derivatives sources remain catalogued but quarantined.

Platform

One SDK across the whole portfolio.

Monolith, Nubia, Nimbus, PrimeDrive, and Prototype emit signed, idempotent events into one queue, one hot store, and one cold lake.
