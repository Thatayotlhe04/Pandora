Terms

Pandora is a data infrastructure layer for portfolio platforms, AI-assisted product experiences, and African-language model training. Platform-specific terms may add product-specific rules.

Model Training

By using a Pandora-enabled platform, users agree that eligible `model_training` events may be used to build, evaluate, distribute, or commercialize datasets and models unless the user opts out.

Opt-Out Effect

Opting out stops future model-training collection for that user. Product improvement metadata may continue where needed to operate the service, but raw content is rejected under the internal-only scope.

Public Sources

Public datasets must preserve attribution, license, source URL, and transformation metadata. Restricted, non-commercial, no-derivatives, or evaluation-only sources are excluded from externally distributed Pandora dataset cuts unless separately approved.
