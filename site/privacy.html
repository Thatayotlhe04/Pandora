Privacy Policy

Pandora collects platform events, AI interaction events, partner-provided records, and approved public-source records to operate data infrastructure that improves AI output through the Pandora SDK and API.

Scopes

product_improvement is internal operational metadata. model_training is default-on and may include eligible content for externally distributable datasets unless a user opts out.

Opt Out

Users can opt out of model_training at any time. Once opted out, SDKs stop sending model-training events for that user and ingestion respects the consent boundary.

Public Sources

Public-source records retain source IDs, upstream URLs, license names, attribution fields, and import provenance so downstream dataset cards can explain exactly where each row came from.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to privacy@pandora.local.
