Privacy Policy

Pandora collects platform events, AI interaction events, and approved public-corpus records to operate products, improve reliability, and build model-training datasets where the user has not opted out.

Consent Scopes

`product_improvement` is internal metadata only. `model_training` is default-on and may include content for Atlas training or externally distributable datasets unless a user opts out.

Opt Out

Users can opt out of `model_training` at any time. Once opted out, SDKs stop sending model-training events for that user and ingestion respects the consent boundary.

Public Corpora

Public-corpus records retain source IDs, upstream URLs, license names, attribution fields, and import provenance so downstream dataset cards can explain exactly where each row came from.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to privacy@pandora.local.
