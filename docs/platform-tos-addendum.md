# Pandora Data Use Addendum

Use this language as the baseline for each platform's Terms of Service and data
notice. It is product/legal copy, not a substitute for jurisdiction-specific
legal review.

## Data Use

We may collect and process usage data, interaction data, prompts, messages,
responses, search queries, corrections, feedback, and related metadata generated
when you use the platform. We use this data to operate the platform, improve
product quality, detect abuse, support analytics, and develop, train, evaluate,
commercialize, distribute, or sell datasets and machine-learning systems.

## Model Training Scope

The `model_training` scope is enabled by default. Data in this scope may include
raw user-provided content and AI-generated responses. This scope is the only
Pandora scope that may be packaged into externally distributed or saleable
datasets or used to train models intended for external commercialization.

## Internal Product Improvement

The `product_improvement` scope is internal only. It is used for analytics,
debugging, safety, reliability, and platform improvement. Raw content fields are
not accepted under this scope by Pandora ingestion.

## Opt Out

Users may opt out of the `model_training` scope at any time from account settings
or by contacting support. Opting out stops future `model_training` collection for
that user after normal processing delays. Opting out does not remove access to the
platform and does not affect internal `product_improvement` processing needed to
operate and improve the service.

## Attribution And Public Corpora

Pandora may also use public or licensed language datasets, including African
indigenous-language corpora, where the source license permits the intended use.
Public-corpus imports should preserve attribution, license, and source metadata.
